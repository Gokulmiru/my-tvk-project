Thalapathy TVK refers to actor-turned-politician Vijay, who is nicknamed "Thalapathy" (Commander) and founded the political party Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK). After retiring from films, Vijay launched the TVK in February 2024 to contest the 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly elections. He was officially declared the party's Chief Ministerial candidate in July 2025. Following a tragic crowd crush at a September 2025 rally, Vijay pledged to prioritize people's safety.
